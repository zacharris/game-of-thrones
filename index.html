The following characters are main characters in the HBO series Game of Thrones. This popular television show is wrapping up it's second of hopefully many seasons. The show draws an extreme amount of viewers and feeds off of viewers desires to relive the dark ages in times of royalty, castles, and dragons.

Eddard StarkLord of Winterfell, Lord Paramount of the North, and Warden of the North. Played by Sean Bean. Eddard was imprisoned in the end of Season 1 and the new King at the time Joffrey Baratheon, had him beheaded as a lesson to show what happens to people guilty of treason.

Fast Facts about Eddard Stark:
  1. Eddard Stark was made Lord of Winterfell by his friend King Robert Baratheon.
  2. Eddard Stark fathered a son while away from home with another woman.
  3. Sean Bean played a main role in the entire first season.
  4. Eddard Stark was beheaded at the end of the first season.
